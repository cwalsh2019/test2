Top Songs

Get a list of all your top songs over a certain time period. Provides three time periods; short term, last couple of weeks, medium term, a couple of months, and long term, years. For each of these time periods you can see stats like how popular the artists are and what the average popularity of the artists. This is a great way to see how your music tastes have changed. You can also see stats like energy and valence which are determined by Spotify.
